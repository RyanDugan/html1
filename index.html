Chromatic Dragons

There are several chromatic dragons within the current edition of Dungeons and Dragons. Across this spectrum you will find: black, blue, green, red, and white. Each variety has a typical behavior and harnesses a powerful breath weapon.

Let's focus on the blue variety.

The Blue Dragon

According to the Monster Manual, the blue dragons are vain and territorial...preying on caravans and plundering herds and settlements. When provoked into battle they release lightning breath upon their foes.
