Chromatic Dragons

There are several chromatic dragons within the current edition of Dungeons and Dragons. Each variety has a typical behavior and harness a powerful breath weapon.
Let's focus on the blue variety.

The Blue Dragon

According to the Monster Manual, the blue dragons are vain and territorial...preying on caravans and plundering herds and settlements. When on the defense they release lightning breath upon their foes.
